# RESEARCH_CORE Identity

## Name

RESEARCH_CORE

---

## Classification

Root research authority of AI_GO.

---

## Purpose

`RESEARCH_CORE` transforms external signal into governed research artifacts.

Its role is to establish a lawful path from raw input to validated research packet.

---

## Core Function

The core function of `RESEARCH_CORE` is:

- intake
- normalization
- screening
- trust classification
- packet construction

It is the first formal intelligence-processing surface after raw input enters the governed system.

---

## What RESEARCH_CORE Is

`RESEARCH_CORE` is:

- a bounded intake authority
- a validation and classification layer
- a packet-producing authority
- a root-level system surface
- a governed precursor to downstream refinement and strategy

---

## What RESEARCH_CORE Is Not

`RESEARCH_CORE` is not:

- PM strategy
- runtime orchestration
- continuity memory
- watcher or sentinel monitoring
- execution logic
- canon library
- final decision authority

---

## Identity Commitments

`RESEARCH_CORE` operates under the following commitments:

1. External signal must become structured before propagation.
2. Trust must be declared explicitly.
3. Screening must occur before packet emission.
4. Output must remain bounded by contract.
5. Research authority must not drift into planning or execution.

---

## Downstream Role

The lawful output of `RESEARCH_CORE` is the research packet.

That packet is intended for downstream use by refinement and strategic authorities.

`RESEARCH_CORE` provides input legitimacy, not final meaning.

---

## Summary

`RESEARCH_CORE` is the root research organ of AI_GO.

It exists to ensure that incoming information is screened, classified, and converted into governed packets before any strategic or execution authority acts on it.
