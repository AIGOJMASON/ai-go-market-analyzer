# PM to Child Core Interface

## Purpose

This interface preserves the lawful relationship between `PM_CORE` and child-core execution surfaces within AI_GO.

It exists so strategic planning outputs may become downstream execution context without collapsing PM authority into direct execution.

---

## Interface Rule

`PM_CORE` may hand downstream planning outputs to child cores only as governed inheritance artifacts.

Raw planning intent, hidden execution logic, or undeclared routing may not cross this boundary.

---

## What May Cross the Boundary

The lawful handoff artifact is the inheritance packet.

At minimum, it preserves:

- PM origin
- target child core
- planning summary
- strategic signal
- scope
- tags
- created timestamp
- inheritance status

---

## What May Not Cross the Boundary

The following may not cross as if they were valid handoff:

- direct PM execution
- runtime ownership
- canon authority
- continuity ownership
- hidden undeclared execution steps

---

## Summary

This interface preserves the distinction between strategic planning authority and downstream child-core execution.
