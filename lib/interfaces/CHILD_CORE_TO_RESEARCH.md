# Child Core to Research Interface

## Purpose

This interface preserves the lawful relationship between child-core execution surfaces and `RESEARCH_CORE` within AI_GO.

It exists so child cores may consume governed research influence or reference research provenance without acquiring research authority.

---

## Interface Rule

A child core may receive research influence only through governed research packets, inherited planning shaped by research, or declared downstream research references.

Raw intake, screening, and trust classification remain outside child-core authority.

---

## What May Cross the Boundary

The following may lawfully move toward child-core execution:

- governed research packet references
- research-derived planning context
- source-aware summaries
- archived research references relevant to execution context

---

## What May Not Cross the Boundary

The following may not be delegated to a child core:

- raw research intake ownership
- screening ownership
- trust classification ownership
- live research packet issuance
- root research governance

---

## Summary

This interface preserves the distinction between governed research authority and downstream child-core execution.
