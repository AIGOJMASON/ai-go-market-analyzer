# Root Authority Map

## Canon Authorities
- CORE: runtime orchestration authority
- SMI: continuity authority
- WATCHER: artifact and transition verification authority
- SENTINEL: long-range drift and instability monitoring
- RESEARCH_CORE: intake, screening, trust, packet emission
- PM_CORE: strategic inheritance authority
- ENGINES: constrained interpretation engines
- LIB: canon, contracts, handoffs, archive reference authority
- CHILD_CORES: governed domain execution authorities

## Law
Every authority must declare:
- what it receives
- what it emits
- what it references
- what it must never bypass

## Single-loader law
Only core/runtime/loader.py may read startup authority files during bootstrap.
