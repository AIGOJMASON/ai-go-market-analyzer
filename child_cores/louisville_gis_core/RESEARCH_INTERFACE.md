# LOUISVILLE GIS CORE RESEARCH INTERFACE

## What it is

Governed research intake interface for `louisville_gis_core`.

## Why it is there

Defines the bounded domain-research surface for Louisville GIS so later domain-specific research can enter the child core lawfully instead of bypassing PM or child-core policy.

## Where it connects

- `AI_GO/child_cores/louisville_gis_core/research/research_interface.py`
- `AI_GO/child_cores/louisville_gis_core/DOMAIN_POLICY.md`
- `AI_GO/child_cores/louisville_gis_core/DOMAIN_REGISTRY.json`

---

## Core Rule

Louisville GIS research intake may describe Louisville GIS domain questions and domain evidence only.

It may not:
- override PM inheritance truth
- act as a general research core
- accept out-of-domain payloads as lawful Louisville GIS work

---

## Allowed Domain Research Themes

- Louisville business location intelligence
- GIS opportunity mapping
- contractor and local market geography
- route, parcel, and location context
- domain-bounded civic or commercial mapping questions
