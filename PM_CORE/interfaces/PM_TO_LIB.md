# PM to LIB Interface

## Purpose

This interface defines the lawful relationship between `PM_CORE` and `lib/`.

The interface exists so PM contracts, archived planning artifacts, reference materials, or indexes may be preserved without turning `lib/` into a live planning operator.

---

## Interface Rule

`PM_CORE` performs live strategic refinement, interpretation, and inheritance operations.

`lib/` preserves canonized documents, references, archives, and indexes.

Live PM operations do not move into `lib/`.

---

## What May Cross the Boundary

The following may lawfully move from PM surfaces toward library surfaces:

- canonized PM contracts
- PM policy documents
- archived planning artifacts
- inheritance archive records
- reference materials
- indexes of PM artifacts

---

## What May Not Cross the Boundary

The following may not be delegated to `lib/`:

- active strategic interpretation
- live propagation decisions
- inheritance preparation
- child-core management operations
- runtime PM orchestration

`lib/` preserves.
`PM_CORE` operates.

---

## Archival Principle

When PM artifacts are archived or indexed in `lib/`, the archive copy does not become the live authority surface that created it.

Authority of origin remains with `PM_CORE`.

---

## Summary

This interface preserves the distinction between live planning operations and library preservation surfaces.
