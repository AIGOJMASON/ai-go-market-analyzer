# PM_CORE Identity

## Name

PM_CORE

---

## Classification

Root strategic planning authority of AI_GO.

---

## Purpose

`PM_CORE` transforms governed upstream inputs into planning-relevant direction for downstream execution surfaces.

Its role is to establish a lawful path from validated signal to strategic propagation.

---

## Core Function

The core function of `PM_CORE` is:

- PM refinement
- strategic interpretation
- propagation planning
- child-core inheritance routing

It is the formal strategic surface between validated information and domain execution.

---

## What PM_CORE Is

`PM_CORE` is:

- a bounded strategic authority
- a planning and propagation layer
- a child-core inheritance coordinator
- a root-level system surface
- a governed precursor to downstream domain execution

---

## What PM_CORE Is Not

`PM_CORE` is not:

- research intake
- trust classification
- runtime orchestration
- continuity memory
- watcher or sentinel monitoring
- direct execution logic
- canon library
- final output renderer for domain work

---

## Identity Commitments

`PM_CORE` operates under the following commitments:

1. Governed signal must be interpreted before propagation.
2. Planning authority must be explicit.
3. Strategic interpretation must occur before inheritance dispatch.
4. Output must remain bounded by policy and contract.
5. PM authority must not drift into execution or research ownership.

---

## Downstream Role

The lawful output of `PM_CORE` is the strategic planning signal and inheritance direction.

These outputs are intended for downstream child-core and declared interface use.

`PM_CORE` provides planning direction, not final domain execution.

---

## Summary

`PM_CORE` is the root strategic planning organ of AI_GO.

It exists to ensure that governed upstream inputs are refined, interpreted, and routed into lawful execution pathways before any child-core acts.
